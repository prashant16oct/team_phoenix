"A father's love can save a life... or take one," sets the tone for a dark and gritty series.


Director:

Mayank Sharma


Stars:

Abhishek Bachchan, Amit Sadh, Nithya Menenl, Ivana Kaur, Shrikant Verma, Saiyami Kher, Plabita Borthakur, Hrishikesh Joshi, Shradha Kaul, Shruti Bapna, Resham Shrivardhan, Shataf Figar & More.



StoryLine

A 6-year-old is kidnapped by a mysterious masked man, who demands an unusual ransom. To save his daughter, Dr. Avinash Sabharwal must kill someone.


Meanwhile, Kabir Sawant's journey continues in the hostile environment of the Delhi Crime branch. Lies, deceit and mind games begin when Kabir is given charge of the investigation, and meets Avinash. How far will Avinash go to save his daughter?



Trivia

Both Amit Sadh and Abhishek Bachchan were part of the Sarkar trilogy. Abhishek Bachchan essayed the role of Shankar Nagre in Sarkar (2005) and Sarkar Raj (2008), while Amit Sadh was in Sarkar 3 (2017) essaying the role of Shivaji "Chiku" Nagre, Shankar Nagre's nephew.
