In the aftermath of a child's disappearance, Dark follows characters from the fictional German town of Winden as they pursue the truth.


Creators:

Matt Duffer, Ross Duffer


Stars:

Millie Bobby Brown, Finn Wolfhard, Winona Ryder


StoryLine

When a young boy disappears, his mother, a police chief and his friends must confront terrifying supernatural forces in order to get him back.



Trivia


The date November 12th is also a critical date in Back to the Future (1985), it being a date in which many time-traveling decisions and actions collide.
