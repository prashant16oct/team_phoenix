Chintu Tyagi is an ordinary, middle class man who finds himself torn between his wife and another woman.


Director:

Mudassar Aziz


Stars:

Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar, Ananya Panday, Aparshakti Khurana, Rajesh Sharma, K.K. Raina, Geeta Agrawal Sharma & More.


StoryLine

Abhinav "Chintu" Tyagi is an ordinary, middle-class man from Kanpur, and a government engineer in the Public Works Department. He has been married to Vedika Tripathi (Bhumi Pednekar), a physics tuition teacher from Lucknow, for three years (according to the time in the story).



Chintu is happy in Kanpur, but he is harassed by his controlling parents for not planning a child, and by Vedika who wants to move to a big city. He often seeks advice from his PWD colleague and childhood friend Fahim Abdul Rizvi.



Trivia

All 3 lead actors work with each other for the first time.
