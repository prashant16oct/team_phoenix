While on her way from Chandigarh to Amritsar to sabotage her own wedding, a woman encounters a stranger who spends the day with her despite their obvious differences.



Director:

Karaan Guliani (as Karaan Gulian)


Stars:

Gippy Grewal, Sargun Mehta, Rajpal Yadav, Dilpreet Dhillon, Ankit Kumar & More.


StoryLine

A Girl from Chandigarh comes to Amritsar with the intention of rejecting a prospective groom and ends up spending the day with a Boy from Amritsar. His constant chatter exasperates her, but they share a curious chemistry nevertheless.



They clash over their cities, their beliefs and ideologies and almost every thing they talk about. Between bouts of culture bashing and despite many scenarios where both could have gone their separate ways, they end-up spending the day together.



Trivia

This is a Remake of a marathi movie.
